Group Project for OutFront Minnesota: Web Navigation and Accessibility Update

Project Overview


Problem:

Many young adults in Minnesota feel isolated from their local LGBTQ+ community because they don't have the resources to connect, learn about and rally around the important issues regarding LGBTQ+ people's rights and safety.

The Solution:

We believe that by updating certain aspects of the OutFront MN website we will be able to encourage community members to check and use the website more often so that they can learn and connect more with the LGBTQ+ community and their counterparts.

Created by:

Malinda Carisch, Sara Hudis, Elise Mary Lanigan

Tools:

Figma, Miro, Invision

  • Our goals for the site based off of our Heuristic Evaluation
  • Changing the layout to make content easier to look at and navigate
  • Improving text accessibility and consistency especially on the website’s buttons
  • Maintaining visual hierarchy/ better clarity on headings and subheadings. Draw the viewer’s eye to the most important page elements
  • Simplifying menu options. Condense top menu from two bars to one.
